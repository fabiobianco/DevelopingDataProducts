App logic descripotion

In this app we apply a Polinomial Regression Model to the Faithful Dataset to predict the Eruption duration. What you will see is that the prediction improves as the degree of the polynomial increases. The Faithful dataset contains details of the eruption duration and the waiting time in between eruptions of the Old Faithful Geyser in Yellowstone National Park in Wyoming, United States (For more details visit https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Old_Faithful).
