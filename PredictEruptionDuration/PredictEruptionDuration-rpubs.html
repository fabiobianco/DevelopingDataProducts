Shiny app logic descripotion

In this Shiny app we apply a Polinomial Regression Model to the Faithful Dataset to predict the Eruption duration. What you will see is that the prediction improves as the degree of the polynomial increases. The Faithful dataset contains details of the eruption duration and the waiting time in between eruptions of the Old Faithful Geyser in Yellowstone National Park in Wyoming, United States.

head(faithful)

  eruptions waiting
1     3.600      79
2     1.800      54
3     3.333      74
4     2.283      62
5     4.533      85

Eruption prediction with 1 degree Polynomial

In this case the relationship between the independent variable x and the dependent variable y is modelled as an 1 degree polynomial in x

waiting_time = 50 # Waiting time for eruption prediction
pol_degree1 = 1   # Polinomial degree

as.numeric(results$fit[1])  # Eruption duration predicted

[1] 1.907381
